Nice, France

Amazing things about the city

Nice boasts one of the most stunning coastal landscapes in the world, with the bright blue Mediterranean Sea contrasting against the white pebble beaches.
The Promenade des Anglais, a seafront walkway, is perfect for strolling, cycling, or simply relaxing and enjoying the view.
The city is a perfect base for exploring other parts of the French Riviera and even for a quick trip to Monaco or Italy, which are nearby.
Nice enjoys a mild Mediterranean climate year-round, making it an ideal destination in any season. Winters are mild, and summers are warm and sunny.
